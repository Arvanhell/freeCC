Saving Private Ryan (1998) is a landmark WWII film directed by Steven Spielberg that follows Captain John Miller (Tom Hanks) and a squad of U.S. soldiers during the 1944 Normandy invasion. Their mission is to find and bring home Private James Ryan (Matt Damon), whose three brothers were killed in action

Moral Dilemma: The squad questions the fairness of risking eight men's lives to save one, forcing them to find personal meaning in their mission.
